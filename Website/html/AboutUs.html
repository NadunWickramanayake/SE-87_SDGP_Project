About us

We are group 'Excalibur - SE GROUP 87 ' . Second-year undergraduates undertaking the BEng (Hons) Software Engineering Degree program at the Informatics Institute of Technology affiliated with the University of Westminster, United Kingdom. We are focused on a depression screening application. The application will consist of a questionnaire and a chatbot, and the data gathered using these two methods will be used to make the diagnosis. The chatbot used in this process will enable users to freely communicate with the app. And the chatbot will be able to mimic the structure of a normal conversation which will in return help to make the user feel comfortable and relax. The conversation between the chatbot and the user will be recorded and will be used for the diagnosis process.
