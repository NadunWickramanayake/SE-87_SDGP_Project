Our Services

Mental health services refer to a range of resources and interventions a imed at promoting and maintaining mental well-being, treating mental health disorders, and supporting individuals experiencing mental health challenges. These services can include quick screening, indepth screening and generating reports of to identify the level of depression..

Quick Screening

Providing a set of questions to the user and using their answers generate the depression level of the user..

Indepth Screening

Provide a chatbot feature and user can share their feeling through that. Then using that users answers generate the depression level of the user.

Generating Reports

Using chatbot feature generate the report about users depression level.
